## Scene Summary

Tom awakens injured and memory-fractured in the rain-soaked streets of Aurora while unknown hunters pursue him. A mysterious watch pulses with awareness, and a holographic woman named Samantha emerges to guide him through the city. During their escape, Samantha reveals familiarity with Tom that he does not understand. They eventually arrive near The Orion, where Tom experiences a sudden intrusive memory involving Dominic Voss and Samantha’s first meeting.
